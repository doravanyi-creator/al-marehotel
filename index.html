Our Ethos

The Sea as a Sanctuary

Located on the pristine edges of the Mediterranean, AL-MARÉ is more than a hotel—it is a dialogue between architecture and the horizon. We believe the sound of the waves is the most potent therapy we offer.

Our suites are designed to invite the sea air in, using sustainable materials that mirror the limestone cliffs and turquoise waters of our private cove.
